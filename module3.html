The Overview


In this module, you will learn the legal processes and ethics in the lifeguarding profession.


  • What is an emergency action plan (EAP)?
  • What are the components of an EAP?
  • How do lifeguards activate an EAP?
  • What happens if lifeguards do not follow an EAP?
  • How do you follow an EAP?
  • What accidents and/or incidents do we have to document?
  • How do you document an accident and/or incident?
  • How do you get patron testimony and witnesses?
  • Are lifeguards responsible for answering police and press (media) inquiries?
  • What are the procedures for handling police and press (media) inquiries?
  • What happens if a patron refuses consent?
  • What do lifeguards do if a patron refuses consent?
  • What is negligence?
  • Why are lifeguards mandated reporters?
  • What is the Good Samaritan Law?
  • What is confidentiality?
  • How do we report a situation?
  • What is abandonment?


Through reading, writing, and participating as a class, we will explore these questions.
