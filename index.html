Course Objectives

Objective One

To understand the lifeguarding profession as a pathway.

Objective Two

To learn how professional lifeguards serve patrons.

Objective Three

To understand the
legal aspects and legal processes of the lifeguarding profession.

Objective Four

To learn about
the components of a lifeguard system.

Objective Five

To demonstrate
rescue and medical techniques, processes and methods.

Objective Six

To understand the unique characteristics
of lifeguarding at
waterfronts.
