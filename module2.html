The Overview


In this module, you will learn how lifeguards understand, support, and work with patrons.


  • What are strategies for working with patrons and families?
  • What are tensions between lifeguards and patrons?
  • What is difference?
  • What is diversity?
  • Is there such a thing as privilege?
  • What are the misconceptions of difference?
  • What are the misconceptions of diversity?
  • What does race have to do with lifeguarding?
  • What are racial microaggressions?
  • What are the the types of racial microaggressions?
  • What is a family?
  • What are the different types of parenting styles?
  • How do we use language to communicate with patrons?
  • How should we approach and talk to patrons?
  • What does it mean to “parent?”


Through reading, writing, and participating as a class, we will explore these questions.
