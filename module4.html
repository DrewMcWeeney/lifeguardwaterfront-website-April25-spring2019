The Overview


In this module, you will learn lifeguarding protocols.


  • How do we create rules?
  • What are challenges with lifeguarding activities, special attractions, and organized groups?
  • What are the types of activities that can happen when lifeguarding?
  • What are consequences?
  • What are the types of consequences?
  • How do we enforce rules?
  • How do we use a whistle and radio?
  • What are the responsibiliies of a facility's management?
  • What should your facility's management inform you of before and during your employment as a lifeguard?
  • What injury prevention strategies do lifeguards use?
  • How do lifeguards handle weather conditions?
  • How can lifeguards contribute to the safety of organized groups?
  • How should lifeguards handle organized groups?
  • How do lifeguards conduct a swim test?
  • What is a swim test used for?
  • How do you lifeguard activities, special attractions and organized groups?


Through reading, writing, and participating as a class, we will explore these questions.
