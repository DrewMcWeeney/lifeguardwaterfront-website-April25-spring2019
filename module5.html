The Overview


In this module, you will learn how to lifeguard a water environment.


  • How do lifeguards scan different water areas?
  • What is total coverage? What is zone coverage?
  • What is scanning? What is watching?
  • How do lifeguards rotate shifts?
  • How do you recognize a victim and/or swimmer who needs help?
  • How do you recognize the difference between a swimmer who needs help and a swimmer who is just playing/enjoying themselves?
  • What is the drowning process?
  • What does the drowning process entail?
  • What are the types of victims?
  • What are the challenges in scanning?


Through reading, writing, and participating as a class, we will explore these questions.
